Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 30-45 minutes depending on your location. We'll provide you with an estimated delivery time when you place your order.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver within a 10-mile radius of our restaurant. Enter your address at checkout to confirm delivery availability.

Can I customize my order?

Yes! You can add special instructions or notes when placing your order. We'll do our best to accommodate your preferences.

What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, we accept cash on delivery. Online payment options will be available soon!

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We have a variety of vegetarian and vegan options available. Check our menu for details.
