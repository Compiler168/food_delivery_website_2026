About FoodUp

Passionate about food, dedicated to quality

Our Story

FoodUp was founded in 2024 with a simple vision: to bring authentic, delicious food to your doorstep. What started as a passion for cooking has grown into a beloved food delivery service serving our community.

Every dish we create is made with love, using fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes. We believe that great food has the power to bring people together and create memorable moments.

Founded by Majid Iqbal, our kitchen combines traditional cooking methods with modern flavors to create a unique dining experience that celebrates the art of good food.
